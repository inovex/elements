# Typography

## Displays

 * Short, important text or numerals; work best on Large Screens;
 * (optional: choosing a more expressive font e.g. handwritten - script style)

<h1 class="display">
  D1 Display Large
</h1>
  <h2 class="display">
  D2 Display Medium
</h2>
  <h3 class="display">
  D3 Display Small
</h3>


## Headlines

**H1 - H3**
- Short
- high-emphasis text on smaller screens
- important regions of content
- _optional: can also use an expressive  typeface_

**H4 - H6**
- Usage for medium-emphasis
- short text
- divide secondary passages of text or secondary content

<h1>
  H1 Headline Large
</h1>
    <h2>
  H2 Headline Medium
</h2>
    <h3>
  H3 Headline Small
</h3>
    <h4>
  H4 Title Large
</h4>
    <h5>
  H5 Title Medium
</h5>
    <h6>
  H6 Title Small
</h6>
